About JICOM

A Trusted Global Ship Brokerage Venture

How We Work

Simple Process. Strong Results.

We follow a structured brokerage workflow—so you always know what’s happening next.

  • 1) Inquiry & Shortlist

    Understand requirement, vessel search, and shortlist selection.

  • 2) Verification & Due Diligence

    Confirm specs, class, documents, and operational status where applicable.

  • 3) Negotiation & Contracts

    Offer handling, valuation guidance, MoA coordination and documentation.

  • 4) Survey, Delivery & Closing

    Support surveys, delivery terms, and final settlement process.
